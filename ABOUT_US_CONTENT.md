## 2. Our Journey (Timeline Section)

### Timeline Introduction

**Title**: Our Journey

**Subtitle**: A story of growth, impact, and unwavering commitment to serving humanity

### Timeline Events

#### 1998 - The Beginning
Chiranjeevi Charity Trust was founded with a vision to serve humanity. The journey began with a small team of dedicated volunteers committed to making a difference in people's lives.

#### 2000-2005 - Blood Bank Initiative
Launched our first major initiative - the Blood Bank program. Over the years, we've collected and distributed over 10 lakh units of life-saving blood, saving countless lives across India.

#### 2005-2010 - Eye Bank Expansion
Established the Eye Bank program, performing over 10,000 corneal transplants and restoring vision to thousands of visually challenged individuals. This milestone marked our commitment to comprehensive healthcare.

#### 2010-2015 - Community Welfare Programs
Expanded our reach with comprehensive community welfare programs, including education support, medical assistance, and disaster relief initiatives. We touched over 100,000 lives during this period.

#### 2015-2020 - Hospital for Movie Workers
Initiated the construction of a dedicated hospital in Chitrapuri, Hyderabad, to provide free primary healthcare for movie workers. This project represents our commitment to supporting those who entertain and inspire us.

#### 2020-2021 - COVID-19 Relief - Oxygen Banks
During the pandemic, we established 32 oxygen banks across Telugu states, saving thousands of lives. Provided ₹7 lakhs worth of accident insurance and 50% subsidy in diagnostics, supporting 15,000+ daily wage film workers.

#### 2021-Present - Digital Transformation & Future Goals
Embracing technology to reach more people, we've launched digital platforms for donations, volunteer registration, and program management. Our future goals include expanding to 50+ cities, establishing 100+ healthcare centers, and impacting 10 million lives by 2030.

### Looking Forward

As we look to the future, our commitment remains unwavering. We envision expanding our reach to serve even more communities, establishing sustainable programs that create lasting change, and building a network of compassion that spans across India. Together, we will continue to transform lives, restore hope, and build a brighter tomorrow for all.

## 6. UI/UX & Animation Instructions

### Design Philosophy

The About Us page is designed to be:
- **Visually Engaging**: Modern, clean design with smooth animations
- **Emotionally Connecting**: Warm imagery and inspiring content
- **User-Friendly**: Intuitive navigation and clear information hierarchy
- **Professional**: Trust-building design with credibility

### Animation Guidelines

#### Scroll-Triggered Animations
- **Fade-ins**: Smooth opacity transitions as elements enter viewport
- **Slide-ups**: Content slides up from below with fade
- **Staggered Animations**: Sequential reveals for multiple items
- **Text Reveals**: Text reveals from bottom with overlay mask

#### Timeline Animations
- **Vertical Timeline**: Animated line connecting events
- **Event Cards**: Alternating left-right reveal animations
- **Image Zoom**: Images zoom in on scroll trigger
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Events reveal as user scrolls

#### Micro-Interactions
- **Hover Effects**: Cards lift, images zoom, icons scale
- **Button Animations**: Bounce, shine, and press effects
- **Smooth Transitions**: 300-450ms transitions throughout
- **Parallax Backgrounds**: Subtle depth with background elements

### Visual Style

#### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Teal (#1a3a3a) - Trust and professionalism
- **Secondary**: Mint (#b8f4d3) - Hope and growth
- **Accents**: Various gradients for timeline events
- **Backgrounds**: White and soft gray gradients

#### Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, large (40px - 72px)
- **Body**: Readable, relaxed line-height (18px - 20px)
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size differentiation
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace for readability

#### Layout
- **Sections**: Full-width with max-width containers
- **Grids**: Responsive 1-3 column layouts
- **Spacing**: Consistent padding and margins
- **Images**: Rounded corners, shadows, hover effects

#### Responsive Design
- **Mobile**: Single column, reduced padding
- **Tablet**: 2-column grids where applicable
- **Desktop**: Full multi-column layouts
- **Breakpoints**: 768px, 1024px

### User Experience Features

1. **Smooth Scrolling**: Enabled for section navigation
2. **Visual Anchors**: Clear section breaks and dividers
3. **Progressive Disclosure**: Information revealed on scroll
4. **Call-to-Actions**: Prominent buttons at strategic points
5. **Loading States**: Smooth content loading
6. **Accessibility**: WCAG AA compliant colors and contrast

### Implementation Notes

- All animations use Intersection Observer for performance
- Images use Next.js Image component for optimization
- CSS custom properties for consistent theming
- Data attributes for animation configuration
- GPU-accelerated transforms for smooth animations
